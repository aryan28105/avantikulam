Welcome to Avantikulam

Avanticulam NGO in Bhopal is a student-driven initiative dedicated to making quality education accessible to all. Founded by MANIT’s INSPIRE Club, it provides free study materials, mentorship, and guidance for JEE and NEET aspirants, ensuring financial constraints don’t limit dreams.

More than just academics, Avanticulam fosters confidence, resilience, and a supportive community. Through its digital platform, it empowers students with the tools and encouragement they need to overcome challenges and achieve their goals..
